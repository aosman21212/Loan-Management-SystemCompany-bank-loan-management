Key Features

Everything your finance team needs to manage bank borrowings

🏭

Correct Liability Accounting

Disbursement posts Dr Bank / Cr Loan Payable. Each repayment posts Dr Loan Payable + Dr Interest Expense / Cr Bank. Textbook double-entry, automatically.

📈

Amortisation Schedule

Auto-generated repayment table showing principal, interest, EMI and outstanding balance for every instalment — both Flat Rate and Reducing Balance methods.

✅

One-Click Instalment Payment

Pay any instalment with a single click. The journal entry is created and posted immediately. Loan closes automatically when all instalments are settled.

📄

Professional PDF Statement

Print a full loan statement with amortisation table, lender details, instalment history and outstanding balance — ready to attach to audit files.

🏗

Multiple Loan Types

Configure Term Loans, Mortgages, Working Capital, Vehicle Finance — each with its own default interest rate, method, accounts and duration limits.

📖

Full Journal Entry Trail

Every disbursement and repayment is linked to the loan. Smart button shows all journal entries. Complete audit trail for your external auditors.

How It Works

Three simple steps from loan application to repayment

1

Apply & Approve

Create a loan application, select the bank, set the amount, interest rate and duration. Submit for confirmation and management approval.

2

Disburse & Schedule

Click "Receive Loan" to post the disbursement entry. The amortisation schedule is generated automatically for the full loan period.

3

Pay Instalments

Each month, click Pay on the due instalment. Principal and interest journal entries are posted. Loan closes when all instalments are paid.

Built-In Loan Types

Pre-configured for the most common bank borrowings — fully customisable

🏭 Term Loan

Medium to long-term bank financing. Reducing balance method. Up to 60 months. Suitable for business expansion and asset purchase.

🏠 Mortgage Loan

Real estate and property financing. Reducing balance over up to 300 months. Low fixed rate with structured amortisation.

💰 Working Capital Loan

Short-term operational financing. Flat rate method for simple interest calculation. Up to 24 months. Fast approval workflow.

🚘 Vehicle Finance

Vehicle and equipment leasing. Reducing balance over up to 60 months. Track multiple vehicles under separate loan records.
